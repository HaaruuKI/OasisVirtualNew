Two Photos

Brooklyn Street at Night

I walked home from the grocery store one night and suddenly felt like I was in a new place. The puddle and the lights and the mostly empty street with metal fences on the left created a scene that I had never before seen despite this being the 100th or more time that I had walked this route.

Parking Garage

A romantic walk on the (seemingly) deserted Roosevelt Island in the East River, led us to this empty parking garage entrance. Steam emanated from a nearby vent creating a moody atmosphere.
